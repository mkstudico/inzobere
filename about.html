Ese Inzobere AI ikora ite?

Inzobere AI ni umufasha wubatswe hifashishijwe ikoranabuhanga rya Artificial Intelligence (AI), ugamije gutanga ubufasha bwihuse kandi bufatika mu bijyanye n’ubuhinzi n’ubucuruzi mu Rwanda.

Uyu mufasha utanga inama zishingiye ku makuru yizewe, akagufasha kumenya:

  • Uburyo bwo kubyaza umusaruro ubutaka, kumenya uko wahangana n’ibyonza n’indwara z’ibihingwa
  • Inama ku gucunga neza umushinga w’ubucuruzi ukiri muto cyangwa uri kwaguka
  • Guhuza abahinzi n’amakuru ku isoko rihari n’amahirwe yo guteza imbere ibihingwa

Inzobere AI yifashishwa binyuze kuri mudasobwa cyangwa telefoni, ikaba ishobora gusubiza ibibazo mu Kinyarwanda ndetse no gutanga ubuyobozi bujyanye n’ubuzima bwa buri munsi bw’umuntu ushaka gutera imbere.

Intego yacu ni ugufasha buri Munyarwanda kubona amakuru ajyanye n’icyo akora, ku gihe, mu rurimi yumva neza.

Byose bigamije kubaka ubukungu bushingiye ku bumenyi, guhanga udushya no guteza imbere abahinzi n’abacuruzi bato.
